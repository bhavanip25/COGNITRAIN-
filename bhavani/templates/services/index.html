Looking for free resources

We have compiled a wealth of information and resources to better understand your child’s learning challenges and are always adding new resources to this page. As a parent, you’re always looking for answers to help your child. The internet provides millions of potential solutions, some are sound and proven, some are not. To help you filter out the bad from the good solutions, Learning Foundations put together a list of valuable resources for parents, teachers, and anyone else trying to help a struggling child. Please bookmark this resources page and refer to it often.
